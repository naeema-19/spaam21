Talk on Reaserch Oppurtunities

Mr Akshat Kumar

Research is the "creative and systematic work undertaken to increase the stock of knowledge", according to Wikipedia. It is indeed researchers who push the boundaries, innovate, and break our limits as a society. But it is often neglected as a career choice. We believe that it is due to the lack of awareness. Our speaker will tell you why research is important and all about research opportunities available today.
