ABOUT

Today, the technical skills of a person are given a lot of importance in a professional career. Oftentimes, all other aspects of a person are neglected and technical skills are considered the only criteria for securing your career. Student Professional Awareness Activities Month (SPAAM) is a month-long global event intended to assist students in gaining professional cognizance thereby bringing the gravity of non-technical aspects of engineering to the spotlight. Whatever be your goals, or your career path, we’ve brought opportunities for you. Why should you participate in SPAAM 2021? Our events will enable you to inflate your decision-making ability, set goals, improve your non-technical skills, along with upgrading your academic performance. You will have the opportunity to interact with an eminent panel of speakers from diverse backgrounds. We have a set of exciting competitions that will help gain the skills you need as a professional to prepare you for this competitive world.
So bide your time for SPAAM 2021!!
